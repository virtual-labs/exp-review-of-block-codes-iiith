Instructions
    Quick theory overview:
  • The minimum distance is defined as $$ d_{min} = \min_{i \neq j} d(c_i, c_j) $$ where \(c_i\) and \(c_j\) are codewords in the code, and \(d(c_i, c_j)\) is the Hamming distance between the codewords \(c_i\) and \(c_j\).

    Procedure:
  • Enter all the codewords and corresponding hamming weights for the given generator matrix. The set of entered codewords will be displayed in the observations section.
  • Enter the minimum distance of the code. Click on Check to verify the entered minimum distance.
  • Click on Reset to reset the entered codewords and minimum distance.
